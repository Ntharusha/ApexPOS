🛡️ Core Business Operations
Retail Point of Sale (POS): A streamlined interface for processing customer sales and transactions.
Inventory Management: Full control over your stock, including product details, quantities, and low-stock tracking.
Category Management: Organize products into logical groups for easier management and reporting.
Sales History: A dedicated area to view, search, and manage all past transactions.
🛠️ Service & Specialized Modules
Repair Management: End-to-end tracking of repair jobs, from receiving a device ("Add Job") to completion and delivery.
Hire Purchase (HP): Manage installment-based sales, including payment tracking and customer balances.
Reload Services: Integrated system for managing digital reloads or mobile top-ups.
Delivery Tracking: Manage and monitor the status of deliveries for sold products or services.
📊 Analytics & Monitoring
Dashboard: Real-time visual overview of business performance, including daily/weekly/monthly sales stats and brand-wise summaries.
Comprehensive Reports: Generate detailed reports on sales, expenses, and business health.
Notifications: Real-time system alerts for critical events like low stock levels or job updates.
👤 Administration & Support
Registration: Manage customer profiles and potentially staff/user accounts.
Expense Management: Track business costs and overheads to monitor net profitability.
Theme Support: Modern UI with both Light and Dark mode options.
